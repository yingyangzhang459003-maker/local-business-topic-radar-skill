# Compliance Rules

## Evidence standard

Use primary sources for policy, regulation, price, admissions, medical, and financial claims. For other material claims, cite the strongest available dated source and state uncertainty when evidence is incomplete.

## Reject these claims and tactics

- Guaranteed outcomes, including promised academic, health, financial, or business results.
- Fabricated scarcity, urgency, availability, demand, rankings, or discounts.
- Invented customer cases, testimonials, reviews, quotes, or experiences.
- Unsupported health, nutrition, medical, treatment, food-safety, or allergy claims.

## Publication boundary

Respect the Business Profile's `forbidden_claims`. Keep merchant connections factual, and put freshness, evidence, or compliance warnings in `risk_notes`. When a claim cannot be supported safely, remove it or replace it with a bounded, verifiable process description.
